① EC Logo
Placeholder for the Escape Collective logo asset in the header. Makes the content source clear to users. Final asset (SVG or PNG) to be supplied — dashed box indicates logo placement. Tapping could deep-link to escapecollective.com, TBD.
② Article Type Pills
Category tag pulled from the RSS item's category or tag field. Examples shown: Race Report (purple), Stage Preview (blue), Feature (green), News (grey). Colour-coded for fast scanning. Mapping from EC's taxonomy to these pill labels to be defined at implementation.
③ Image vs Compact Cards
Cards with an article image (from RSS <media:content> or <enclosure>) display the image at the top. Articles without images fall back to a compact text-only layout. The feed naturally mixes both — no enforced uniformity needed.
④ Filter
Filter icon opens a sheet to narrow the feed by article type (Race Reports, Previews, Features, News) or by race. Not yet wireframed in detail — included as a placeholder to signal intent. Default state shows all types unfiltered.
⑤ Card Tap
Tapping anywhere on a card opens the full article. Options: (a) in-app browser (keeps the user inside the app), or (b) hand-off to Safari / default browser. Recommendation is in-app browser with a share sheet icon so users can still forward the link. TBD with product.
⑥ RSS Fields Used
Headline → title · Excerpt → description (truncated to 2 lines) · Author → dc:creator · Time → pubDate (relative, e.g. "2h ago") · Image → media:content or enclosure · Pill category → category.
