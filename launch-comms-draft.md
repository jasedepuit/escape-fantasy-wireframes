## New features — high level

**A proper home screen**
No more landing straight on the leaderboard wondering what's happening. The new home screen shows your active competitions at a glance — your current rank, your last pick, what's coming up — plus the latest news and race coverage from Escape Collective.

**Leagues with chat**
Your league now has a group chat built in. Share your picks, debate your selections, rub it in when you're right. You can share your pick as a card directly into the chat and your league mates can react — or commiserate.

**A proper competition browser**
Easily see all active and upcoming competitions, join new ones, and switch between them without getting lost. No more hunting around.

**Better leaderboards**
Fully scrollable, no 100-entry limit, and you can always find yourself. Tap your rank to jump straight to your row. Position changes are shown on every update so you can see who's on the move.

**AI pick suggestions**
Not sure who to pick? Get a data-driven suggestion with a breakdown of why — form, stage profile, value. It's there when you want it, out of the way when you don't.

**Teams-based competition — new format**
This is the big one. Alongside the existing stage-race format (pick one rider per stage), we're launching a whole new way to play Fantasy: build a team of riders for an entire season and rack up points across multiple races. More on this below.

## Teams-based competition — how to play

Starting with the Giro d'Italia, you can now build your own fantasy team and compete across the whole race. Here's how it works:

**Building your team**
- You have a credit budget to spend — use it to assemble a squad of riders within the cap
- Each rider has a price based on their form and expected performance
- Your squad has slots across four rider types: All-Rounders, Climbers, Sprinters, and Unclassed riders, plus a Wildcard slot you can use however you like
- Give your team a name — this is what your league mates will see on the leaderboard
- Your team is locked when the competition starts, so get your picks in before the gun

**Scoring points**
- Your riders score points based on their results in every stage of the race
- Points accumulate across all stages — a strong GC contender can rack up points all the way to the final stage in Paris
- Check your Team Overview to see how each rider is performing and where your points are coming from

**The leaderboard**
- The Teams leaderboard ranks all squads by total points accumulated across the competition
- You can see how every team in your league is doing — and tap any team to see their full roster

**Transfers**
- Transfer windows may open between races during multi-race seasons, letting you make changes to your squad
- Keep an eye on notifications — we'll let you know when a window opens

**Tips for new players**
- Don't blow your whole budget on one big name — depth matters across a long race
- Think about stage profiles: a sprinter won't help you in the mountains
- The Wildcard slot is your flex — use it for a value pick from any category
- Watch the AI suggestion if you're unsure — it won't always be right, but it's a useful starting point
