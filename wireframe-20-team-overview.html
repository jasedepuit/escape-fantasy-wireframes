Screen 20 · Team Overview — Annotations
① Entry Points
Accessed from the View Team → CTA on a Home competition card or the Competition Browser, for Teams-format competitions only. Also the destination after confirming a team on the Team Selection screen (Screen 19). Acts as the primary ongoing view for a Teams competition — the equivalent of My Picks (Screen 03) for Stage Race format.
② Transfer Window Badge
Two states: Closed (grey lock icon, shown here) and Open (green, pulsing lock-open icon). Per the rules, transfer windows occur at regular intervals set by the competition director — typically at rest days. When open, the badge turns green and the bottom CTA becomes active. The Home card's CTA also switches from View Team → to Make Transfer →.
③ Next Transfer Window
Countdown to the next window displayed in the summary card. If no further transfer windows are configured for this competition, this row is hidden. If a window is currently open it shows the window close time instead: Window closes in 18h 40m.
④ Squad List
9 riders listed in class order: All-Rounder → Climber → Sprinter → Unclassed → Wildcard. Left-border colour matches class: AR · CL · SP · UC · WC. Points shown are cumulative across all scored categories (stage finish, GC, sprint classification, KOM). Highest scorer's points are highlighted in orange.
⑤ DNF / Abandoned State
Riders who have abandoned or been disqualified are dimmed and carry a red DNF badge. Their accumulated points stand (per the rules: points gained before a disqualification deadline stand). This is a strong visual signal to act during the next transfer window. When a transfer window is open, a DNF rider's row could offer a direct Replace → shortcut.
⑥ Transfer CTA Button
Two states mirroring the window badge: Locked (grey, disabled, shows next window time) and Open (orange, active — Make Transfer →). Tapping the active button navigates to Team Selection (Screen 19) with the current squad pre-filled, ready for swaps. No automatic substitutions are ever made per the rules.
Summary Stats
Rank with delta arrow (▲/▼ change since last stage). Total Pts with current stage progress. Leader's Pts showing the gap to first place. These three stats give a quick competitive snapshot without needing to visit the leaderboard.
