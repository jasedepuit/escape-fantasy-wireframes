Screen 14 — Modal
Enroll League Sheet
A bottom sheet that appears immediately after a director joins a competition individually. Lets them enroll their directed leagues in the same competition in one step, without navigating to each league's settings separately.
Trigger — Primary
1
Post-join prompt — fires after tapping "Join Competition →" on Screen 02, but only if the user directs at least one league. Non-directors never see this sheet. The competition browser is dimmed behind a 55% black overlay.
Trigger — Secondary
2
"Enroll a league →" CTA on competition cards in Screen 02, visible to directors on competitions they've already joined personally. Tapping opens this same sheet without the success header — just the league checklist. Serves as the fallback if the director dismissed the post-join prompt or joined via a different route.
League List
3
Only leagues where the user is Director are shown — members can't enroll leagues they don't control. All unenrolled leagues are pre-selected (checked) by default to reduce friction; the director taps to deselect any they don't want.
4
Already enrolled leagues are shown greyed out with an "Enrolled" label rather than a checkbox. This confirms to the director that those leagues are already in and prevents double-enrollment confusion.
Button Behaviour
5
Enroll button label updates dynamically: "Enroll 2 Leagues", "Enroll 1 League", "Enroll 3 Leagues". If all leagues are deselected, button becomes disabled. On tap: enrolls selected leagues and dismisses sheet back to the competition browser.
6
Skip for now — dismisses without enrolling. Directors can always enroll later via League Settings (Screen 12) → Competitions, or via the secondary CTA on Screen 02.
This sheet also resolves the question of new competitions opening mid-season — the director sees the prompt when they personally join, rather than having to discover the League Settings path unprompted.
