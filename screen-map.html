Key Cross-Screen Navigation Flows
1
Quick pick from Home
Tapping "Pick Stage N →" on a Home competition card bypasses My Picks and goes directly to Rider Selection for that stage. Home → Rider Selection
2
Competition switcher
Persistent banner on all Compete sub-screens. Tapping opens the Competition Browser without resetting the Compete tab history. Any Compete screen → Competition Browser
3
Article reader → pick flow
Stage preview articles show a "Pick your rider" CTA pinned at the bottom. Tapping goes to Rider Selection for the relevant stage. Article Reader → Rider Selection
4
Rider injury alert
Push notification or warning badge on My Picks taps directly to Rider Selection for the affected stage. Notification / My Picks → Rider Selection
5
Rider profile → pick confirm
Rider Profile Card (modal) always shows a "Pick this rider" CTA that confirms the pick and dismisses the modal back to Rider Selection. Rider Profile → (confirms) → My Picks
6
League chat → pick share
Tapping a pick-share card in League Chat shows that user's rider pick for that stage (read-only Rider Profile). League Chat → Rider Profile (read-only)
7
Score update notification
After stage results, tapping the push notification deep-links directly to Per-Stage Results for that stage. Notification → Per-Stage Results
8
Leaderboard → opponent picks
Tapping any user row on the leaderboard shows their picks for each stage (read-only My Picks view). Leaderboard → My Picks (read-only)
9
Team format join → team selection
Joining a Team Competition from the Competition Browser goes to Team Selection (not My Picks). The two formats branch here. Competition Browser → Team Selection
