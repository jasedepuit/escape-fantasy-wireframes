Three-step summary of Stage Race play. Mirrors the flow in Screens 03 (My Picks) and 04 (Rider Selection). The key concept to land: one pick per stage, before the cut-off. Scoring from actual race classifications keeps it grounded.

Three-step summary of Teams play. Mirrors Screens 19–20. Key concepts: budget cap, 4 rider classes, 9-rider squad, transfer windows. The "Wildcard" slot is not mentioned here to keep it simple — discovered organically during team selection.
